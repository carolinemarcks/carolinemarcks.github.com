A little about me...

I grew up in central Connecticut and have never moved except from school to school. I attended a boarding high school for four years before starting at Tufts University in 2010. Though I always knew I wanted to be an engineer (building things and solving puzzles has always been a passion of mine), I jumped around between many disciplines before settling down in computer science. I have since spent a lot of time exploring parallel computing, and hope to continue work in that field after graduation in 2014.
